This document describes the CSVW Vocabulary and Term definitions used for creating Metadata descriptions for Tabular Data. See [[!tabular-meta-data]] for further information.

The CSV on the Web Working Group was chartered to produce a Recommendation "Access methods for CSV Metadata" as well as Recommendations for "Metadata vocabulary for CSV data" and "Mapping mechanism to transforming CSV into various Formats (e.g., RDF, JSON, or XML)". This document describes the http://www.w3.org/TR/ns/csvw# namespace used in these definitions.

Introduction

<%= defn["dc:description"]["en"] %>

This specification makes use of the following namespaces:

csvw:
http://www.w3.org/ns/csvw#
rdf:
http://www.w3.org/1999/02/22-rdf-syntax-ns#
rdfs:
http://www.w3.org/2000/01/rdf-schema#
xsd:
http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema#
dc:
http://purl.org/dc/terms/
dcat:
http://www.w3.org/ns/dcat#
prov:
http://www.w3.org/ns/prov#
